Our Mission

The AVMS Encyclopaedia Veterinariae is a comprehensive digital repository created by the Association of Veterinary Medical Students (AVMS), ABU Zaria Chapter. Our mission is to provide easy and organized access to academic resources, including lecture notes, handouts, textbooks, and past questions, to support the academic journey of every veterinary student.
